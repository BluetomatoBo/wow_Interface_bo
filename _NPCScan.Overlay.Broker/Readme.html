_NPCScan.Overlay.Broker

This add-on works with _NPCScan.Overlay and gives you a toggle button to show or hide it's Mini Map Overlay display. Use with your LibDataBroker (LDB) tool of choice.

Click: Toggles display of _NPCScan.Overlay's Mini Map layer.
CONTROL-Click: Toggle display of _NPCScan.Overlay's World Map layer.
Middle-Click: Toggle display of Both _NPCScan.Overlay's Mini & World Map layers
Right Click: Open _NPCScan.Overlay's Configuration Options.
